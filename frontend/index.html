How GPA is calculated

Each grade carries quality points (A = 4.0, B+ = 3.3, C = 2.0, and so on, following the HEC 4.0 scale used across Pakistani universities). Your GPA is the sum of (quality points × credit hours) for every subject, divided by your total credit hours.
