About this tool

Why we built this

Most GPA calculators ask you to type in every subject, its credit hours, and grade one by one — tedious when you just want a quick answer. We built this tool so any Pakistani university student can upload a photo of their result card and get their GPA instantly, without any manual data entry.

How it works

Upload a photo of your result card and the tool automatically reads it, extracting each subject along with its credit hours and grade. Every extracted value is shown to you in an editable table, so you can double-check and correct anything before the final GPA is calculated.

Who this is for

This tool is built with Pakistani university students in mind, using the HEC 4.0 GPA scale commonly followed across universities in Pakistan.

No sign-up, no cost

There's no account to create and no data saved after you close the page. Just upload, calculate, and go.

Why it's free

This tool is free to use because it's supported by ads shown on the site, rather than subscriptions or paid accounts. That lets us keep it open to every student without any signup or payment, while covering the running costs of the AI processing and hosting behind the scenes.
